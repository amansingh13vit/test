What is accidental death insurance

Guaranteed universal life insurance : a type of permanent life insurance that offers a guaranteed no lapse rider guaranteeing the policy remains in force even if the cash value drops to zero. Upon the death of the insured, the lump sum death benefit is paid income tax free to the policy beneficiary. The policy can be tailored to end upon a certain age such as 90, 95, 100, 105, 115 or 121.

  • Covers accidental deaths: trip and falls, poisoning (accidental), acts of God (fall into large hole that opened in ground, etc…), does NOT cover suicides, EVER!
  • Does NOT cover natural death, i.e. heart attack, stroke, cancer, etc…
  • Good policy option for specific individuals who do not qualify for anything else.
  • Double indemnity if on a common carrier (plane, train, taxi, boat- commercial, not private).
